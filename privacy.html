2. General Information

The protection of personal data is important. This privacy policy informs you about what data is processed when using the app "Raumigo", for what purposes this is done, and what rights data subjects have.

The app is currently designed so that data is processed locally on the end device.

3. Scope of Processing

The following data categories may be processed during use:

  • 3D scan and geometry data of objects and environments,
  • derived measurement, position, and calculation results,
  • locally stored project, scan, and report data,
  • technical settings within the app, such as language or units of measurement,
  • device permissions for camera, LiDAR, and comparable sensor functions, insofar as these are enabled for use.

4. Purposes of Processing

Processing is carried out exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Provision and technical execution of app functions,
  • Capture, display, and analysis of objects and transport paths,
  • Calculation of possible fit, collision, and movement information,
  • Local storage of projects, scans, and results,
  • Display and management of user-related settings within the app.

5. Legal Basis

Processing is carried out on the basis of Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR, insofar as processing is necessary for the provision of app functions requested by you, and on the basis of Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR, insofar as this is necessary for secure, stable, and user-friendly operation of the app.

6. Camera, LiDAR, and Sensor Access

The app uses the camera, LiDAR, and possibly other device sensors exclusively for capturing and processing spatial information for the intended app functions.

No transmission of this data to servers operated by the controller takes place in the current version of the app.

7. Recipients of Personal Data

No disclosure of personal data to third parties or transmission to servers operated by the controller takes place in the current version of the app.

8. No Analytics, No Tracking, No Advertising

The app does not use any analytics tools, personal tracking, or advertising networks in its current version.

9. Storage and Deletion

Data generated in the app is stored locally on your device. Data remains stored until you delete it within the app or uninstall the app.

If optional cloud, synchronization, backup, account, or server functions are offered in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.

10. Automated Decisions

No automated decision-making within the meaning of Art. 22 GDPR takes place.

11. Rights of Data Subjects

Data subjects have the following rights under applicable law:

  • Right of access,
  • Right to rectification,
  • Right to erasure,
  • Right to restriction of processing,
  • Right to data portability,
  • Right to object to certain processing,
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority.
